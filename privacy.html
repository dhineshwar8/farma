Privacy Policy for FARMA

FARMA is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Digital Farm Management application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and role (Farm Owner, Farmer, Veterinarian, Customer)
  • Farm Details: Farm name, location, size, and operational information
  • Professional Information: Veterinary license numbers, clinic details, and professional certifications
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and security tokens

1.2 Farm Management Data

  • Livestock Information: Cattle identification, health records, treatment history
  • Medical Records: Antimicrobial usage (AMU) data, vaccination records, withdrawal periods
  • Production Data: Milk production records, quality assessments, batch information
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for farm locations and livestock tracking

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App interactions, feature usage, performance metrics
  • Network Information: IP address, connection type, network provider

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Farm Management: Track livestock health, manage treatments, monitor production
  • Compliance Monitoring: Ensure Maximum Residue Limits (MRL) compliance
  • Quality Assurance: Maintain product traceability from farm to consumer
  • Healthcare Management: Facilitate veterinary consultations and emergency access

2.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Analytics: Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and alerts
  • Support: Provide customer service and technical assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and audit trails

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Authorized Sharing

  • Within Farm Network: Share data with authorized farm members based on role permissions
  • Veterinary Access: Provide livestock data to approved veterinarians for treatment purposes
  • Regulatory Bodies: Share compliance data with agricultural and food safety authorities when required
  • Quality Verification: Provide traceability information to consumers via QR code scanning

3.2 Service Providers

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Firebase (Google) for data storage and authentication
  • Analytics Services: Anonymized usage data for app improvement
  • Notification Services: Push notification providers for alerts and updates

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted and stored is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions ensure data access is limited to authorized users
  • Blockchain Integration: Immutable audit trails for critical farm operations
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing

4.2 Data Backup and Recovery

  • Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
  • Disaster recovery procedures to ensure data availability
  • Secure backup storage in multiple geographic locations

5. Data Retention

5.1 Retention Periods

  • Livestock Health Records: Retained for 10 years as per regulatory requirements
  • Treatment Records: Maintained for 7 years for compliance tracking
  • Production Data: Kept for 5 years for quality assurance
  • User Account Data: Retained while account is active plus 2 years

5.2 Data Deletion

Upon account termination or data retention period expiry, personal data will be securely deleted. Anonymized data may be retained for statistical and research purposes.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • Data Access: View and download your personal data
  • Data Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Account Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data

6.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control data sharing preferences within the app
  • Manage notification settings and communication preferences
  • Set privacy levels for different types of farm data

7. Cookies and Tracking

FARMA uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Authentication Tokens: To maintain secure login sessions
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand app usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Preference Storage: To remember your app settings and preferences

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Integrated Services

  • Google Firebase: Database, authentication, and cloud functions
  • Google Maps: Location services and mapping functionality
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. Children's Privacy

FARMA is designed for agricultural professionals and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located in different countries. We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and use appropriate safeguards.

11. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Updates posted on our website

12. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent
  • Contract Performance: To provide our services
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with agricultural regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and ensure security

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • Applicable agricultural data protection regulations
